Hail and well met!
The purpose of this fair site is to lend aid in the teaching of the English tongue unto scholars of high-school degree. Herein shall students acquaint themselves with words of medieval ilk through exercises both lively and interactive. This humble undertaking was wrought by Dorgo Andrei and Leucea Mihnea of the National College “Mihai Eminescu,” Oradea.

Greetings and welcome!
This website is designed to support high school students in learning the English language. Here, learners will explore medieval-style vocabulary through engaging and interactive exercises. This project was created by Dorgo Andrei and Leucea Mihnea from the “Mihai Eminescu” National College in Oradea.
